Computer Saftey Rules

  1. Keep Personal Information Private:
    • I will not share personal information online, such as my full name, address, phone number, school name, or any other sensitive details.
    • I will not share my passwords with anyone, including friends.
  2. Practice Respectful Behavior:
    • I will treat others with kindness and respect while using computers, including online discussions, comments, and social media.
    • I will not use computers to bully, harass, or intimidate others.
  3. Avoid Harmful Content:
    • I will not access, view, or share inappropriate or harmful content, such as violence, adult content, or illegal materials.
    • I will report any inappropriate content that I encounter online to a trusted adult.
  4. Follow Copyright Laws:
    • I will respect the intellectual property of others and not use copyrighted material without proper permission.
    • If I create content, I will properly credit and respect the work of others when necessary.
  5. No Social Media Access in Class::
    • I will not access or use any social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat) during class, including lectures, discussions, and group activities.
  6. Be Cautious with Downloads:
    • I will only download software, files, or apps from trusted sources and avoid downloading anything suspicious or illegal.
  7. Report Any Concerns:
    • I will immediately report any suspicious or uncomfortable online interactions to a trusted adult or teacher.
    • If I encounter cyberbullying or any harmful online behavior, I will speak up and help create a safer environment.
