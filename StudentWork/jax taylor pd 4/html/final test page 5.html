Porsche Panamera Turbo S E-Hybrid Sport Turismo Porsche=expanding its hybrid range with the addition of the new Panamera Turbo S E-Hybrid Sport Turismo. It combines a 4.0L V8 engine with an electric motor for an output of 680 hp and 626 lb-ft of torque. You can expect to see the all-new Panamera to arrive at Porsche Fremont in the spring of 2018.

911 Carrera T=Meet the newest member of the 911 family, the Porsche 911 Carrera T. Porsche is reviving the puristic concept behind the 1968 911 T with this new T model, the lightest out of the 911 family weighing at just 3,142 pounds! This lightweight Porsche features a 3.0L 6-Culinder rear-engine to deliver 370 hp and go from 0-60 mph in just four seconds. Cruise through Newark, Milpitas, San Jose, and all through the East Bay because the 911 Carrera T is available for order now at Porsche Fremont.
