Habitats

African Forest Elephants

African forest elephants inhabit the dense rain forest of west and central Africa

African Savanna Elephants

African savanna elephants mostly inhabit the wooded savannas and grasslands of sub-Saharan Africa

Asian Elephants

Asian elephants are found in isolated pockets of India and Southeast Asia, including Sumatra and Borneo
