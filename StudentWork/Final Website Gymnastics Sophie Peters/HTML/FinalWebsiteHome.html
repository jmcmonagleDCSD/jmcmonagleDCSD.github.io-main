Gymnastics

By: Sophie Peters


Gymnastics is an olympic sport that has four events. For girls, these four events are: Floor, Vault, Uneven Bars, And Balance Beam. For boys, these events are: Floor, Pommel horse, Vault, Horizontal High Bar, Parallel Bars, and Rings. These events need quite a lot of stamina and endurance to do. This website contains information on the girls events: Floor, Beam, Bars, And Vault.

The sport of gymnastics is very fun, but requires a lot of skill. I am a gymnast at Castle Rock Chameleon Gymnastics, (Near The Outlets and King Soopers) My personal favorite event is floor. My best skill on the floor is a roundoff back tuck, ( A cartwheel thingy with a backflip at the end.) I HATE beam. It is very unsafe, and without skill, you could injure yourself, and on a high beam, it could be extremely bad (but this is VERY unlikely, luckily). Speaking of my ranking of events, these are my favorite events listed from favorite to least favorite.

  1. Floor
  2. Vault
  3. Bars
  4. Beam
