Wave

The Edea Wave boot is a good option for begginer skaters with a boot stregth of 40. The Edea Wave boot is meant for performing single jumps. It also has a wave texture adds style to your skating and support while you skate. The starting price of the Edea Wave boot is 265$.

Overture

The Edea Overture is a great option for begginers that may be a bit older and need a stronger boot. The Overture boot has a stregth of 55 and is a good boot for single jumps and axel. The Overture boot is a combination of anatomical and soft padding to help develop your technique. The starting price of the Edea Overture boot is 365$.

Chorus

The Edea Chorus is a good option for serious skaters. The Chorus boot has a stregth of 75 and is a good boot for single and double jumps. The Chorus boot is a great boot for syncro and freestyle skaters. The Edea Chorus boot has a starting price of 485$.

Concerto

The Edea Concerto is a great option for intermediate skaters. The Concerto boot has a stregth of 85 and is a good boot for triple and quad jumps. The Concerto boot is best at supporting freestyle and pairs skaters. The Concerto boot has memory foam to help adapt to your foot skape. The starting price of the Edea Concerto boot is 785$.

Ice fly

The Edea Ice Fly is a great option for strong and serious skaters. The Ice Fly boot has a stregth of 90 and is good for triple and quad jumps. The Ice Fly boot is best at supporting freestyle, pairs, inline, and syncro. The Edea Ice Fly is the lightest boot. The boot also has air tech linging which means that the boot will dry more quickly. The starting price of the Ice Fly is 885$.

Piano

The Edea Piano is a amazing option for elite skaters. The Piano boot has a stregth of 95 and is amazing for triple and quad jumps. The Piano boot is best at supporting freestyle, pairs, and inline skaters. The Piano boot has built in anti-shock which helps with taking away the shock when you land a jump or fall. The starting price of the Piano boot is 995$.
