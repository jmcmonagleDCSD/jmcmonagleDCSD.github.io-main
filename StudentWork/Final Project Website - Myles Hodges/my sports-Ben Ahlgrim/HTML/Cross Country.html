when running Cross Country your age determines the distance that you run. There are certain courses that can be really flat or hilly and it might be rainy or snowy but either way you have to run. there are special shoes for cross country that have holes for spikes that help you stay standing and they can even have a plate in them that propels you

If you are 12-13 you run 3k=2 miles. If you are 14-15 you run 4k=2.5 miles and 16-17 you will run 5k.
