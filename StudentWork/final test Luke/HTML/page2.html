Fishing In The Bahamas

Fish

There are many different kinds of fish in the Bahamas. The inshore fish include Smaller varieties such as yellowtail, kingfish, redfish (drum and sheepshead), snapper, and grouper. The offshore fish include Larger fish such as amberjack, Mako sharks, blackfin, yellowfin, wahoo, marlin, cobia, dorado, king mackerel.
