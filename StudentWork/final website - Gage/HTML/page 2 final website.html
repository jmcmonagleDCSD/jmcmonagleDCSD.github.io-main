D-Day

D-Day (Doomsday) was the begining of the invasion of europe. about 133,000 troops landed on omaha beach on Higgins boats parrachutes and other forms of transport. 4,414 died on D-Day and 5000 were wounded. The United Kingdom, Canada and the U.S contributed to D-Day most of the allies however contributed to the battle of normandy.
