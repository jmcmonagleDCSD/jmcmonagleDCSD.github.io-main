Campus

Stanford is known for having one of the best college campuses. As stated by niche.com Stanford has the 20th best college campus in 2024. There are almost 6,000 colleges and universities in the USA, which makes Stanford in the top 0.033333 perecnt in the country. Stanfords campus is one of the largest in the country, spanning 8,180 acres. Their campus is very unique, as horses use to just roam around the campus as they please like they too were a student. Many people even after college stay around te bay area, near the university in small houses or apartments. An astounding 93% of undergraduates live on campus, and another 60% of graduates. Stanford also has some of the best weather year round.
