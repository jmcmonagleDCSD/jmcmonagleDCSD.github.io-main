When did college baseball start?

The first ever college baseball game was on July 1rst, 1859 in Pittsfield massachuesetts. The game was betwem Amherst college and Williams College. The final score of the game was 73-32 with Amherst coming out on top. This was a monumental day in college baseball history and the beggining of something remarkable.
