Who are the Dirty Heads?

They are a band that lives in california. That consists of 8 people and has had few other members in the past.
The first of these 8 is Dustin Bushnell also know as "Duddy B" that plays the Guitar and the Vocals.
Another within the band is Jared Watson also know as "Dirty J" that does Vocals.
The 3ed on this list is Jon Olazabal that played the drums and now plays other percussion.
The 4th apon the list is David Foral that plays the Bass guitar.
The 5th one were going over is Matt Ochoa that plays the drums.
Yet another person is Shawn Gonzalez that plays the Keyboard and backs up vocals.
The 7th Person were going over is Mark Bush that uses the trumpet and backs up vocals.
Last but not least is Ruben Durazo that plays the Trombone and backs up vocals.
