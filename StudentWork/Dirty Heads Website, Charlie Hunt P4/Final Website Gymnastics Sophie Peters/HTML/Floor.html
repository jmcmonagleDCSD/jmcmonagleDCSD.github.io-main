Floor

Floor is an event where you do not need any objects. The gymnastics floor has think springs under it to give you more bounce when you are tumbling or leaping. On floor, you can do pretty much anything. From cartwheels to roundoff backhandspring double fulls, floor has a wide range of levels of expertise. Floor is my favorite event, and I am very good at it. One thing I would love is to do a floor routine to an instrumental Taylor Swift song like Snow On the Beach or Gold Rush.

Tricks

There are many tricks that you can do on floor, and they are all very fun. Here are some examples:

  • Roundoff Backhandspring Back Tuck
  • Punch Front
  • Aerial
  • Standing back tuck
  • Roundoff Backhandspring pike or double full
