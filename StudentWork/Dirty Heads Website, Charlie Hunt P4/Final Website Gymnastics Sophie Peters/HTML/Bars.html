Bars

Bars is an event where you need a lot of core and arm strength. There are many routines you can do on bars, here is an example: Kip cast double back hip circle, squat on jump to high bar, tap swing, tap swing flyaway dismount. A bar routine takes a long time to learn, and if it is not executed correctly, there can be major deductions at competitions.

That was Simone Biles' bar routine at the 2018 World Championship

As you can see from above, bars is a very cool event to watch, and it it also pretty demanding of strength and endurance. Bars is a great thing to do if you want to work on your arm and core strength.
