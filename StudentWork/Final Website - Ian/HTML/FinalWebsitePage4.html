The Animals

The Earth has many different types of animals from mamals to insects, birds, fish, amphibians, reptiles, mollucks, invertebretes, arachnids,cnadarians, and crsutanstions. Mamals are vertebrates with hair, they give live birth and don't lay eggs. Amphibians are small vertevrates that require water to survive, they lay eggs. Arachnids all have eight legs and don't have antennaes, they lay eggs. Cnidrians are soft-bodied animals that have stingers. Birds are vertebrates animals that have feathers, wings, and beaks, they lay eggs. Fish are aquatic vertebrates that have gills, no limbs, they lay eggs. Mollucks are animals that have a mantle or mass of soft flesh that covers the soft body thay lay eggs. Crustaceans are amimals that have a hard exoskellition to protect their organs, they lay eggs.
