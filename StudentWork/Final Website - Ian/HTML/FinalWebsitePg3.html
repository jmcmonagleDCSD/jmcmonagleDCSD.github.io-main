Fossil Fuels

Pollution is produced by a massive number of things. The thing that produces the most amount of polution is the burning of fossil fuels. Burning fossil fuels releases Carbon Monoxide, Carbon Dioxide, Sulfur, Nitrogen Dioxide, nitric Oxide, Volatile organic compounds, and Hydrocarbons. Out of those 8 harmful chemicals Nitrogen Dioxide is the most dangerous. studies show that increased levels of Nitrogen Dioxide can cause toruble breathing, colapsing, and death.
