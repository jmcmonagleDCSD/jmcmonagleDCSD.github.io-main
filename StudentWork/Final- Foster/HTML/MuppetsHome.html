What is a muppet?

A Muppet can be anythimg. A muppet can be a cup, a frog, a weird creature, or even a coat rack. A muppet usualy has a big mouth and odd eyes. Muppets come in all shapes and sizes. Colors are a major feat for a muppet. Muppets are very colorfull.
