What Lemon Sharks Eat


bony fish

Lemon sharks eat a wide spread of fish such as catfish, mullets, jacks, croakers, porcupine fish, and cowfish

credits to Willy Volk from Flickr

Crustaceans

Lemon sharks eat a wide variety of crustaceans such as crayfish and crabs.

image by rawpixel from rawpixel

Rays

Lemon sharks eat rays too. Some of the rays they eat are stingrays and eagle rays.
